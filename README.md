👨‍💻 About Me
I transform complex technical challenges into production-ready products at the intersection of full-stack development, applied machine learning, and business strategy.

With a strong foundation in both engineering and design, I build end-to-end applications that solve real problems — from AI-powered healthcare screening tools for underserved communities to predictive analytics systems that drive business decisions.

What I bring to the table:

🏗️ Full-Stack Engineering — Building scalable, offline-first PWAs with modern tech stacks

🤖 Applied AI/ML — Developing and deploying models with TensorFlow, scikit-learn, and Gemini API

🎨 UX-Driven Development — Translating user needs into intuitive, accessible interfaces

📊 Data-Driven Strategy — Bridging technical outputs and business impact through clear communication

Currently working on Anemo Check — a non-invasive AI anemia screening PWA designed for the Philippine health sector, combining Google's Gemini/Gemma models with offline-first architecture.
